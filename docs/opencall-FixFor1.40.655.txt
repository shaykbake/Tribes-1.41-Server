This fix is for Tribes 1 clientversion 1.40.655 only!
Lower versions do not need this fix!!!!

Installation:
Backup your opencall and opencall2 folders from your
Tribes 1.40 directory. (e.g. "c:\tribes1.40\")
Unzip the archive to your Tribes 1.40 directory
with directory structure and overwrite existing
files.

Uninstall:
Delete the folders opencall and opencall2 in the
Tribes 1.40 folder, then restore your backup.

For additional Info about the problems with maps
and client 1.40 check out:
http://www.tribalwar.com/forums/showthread.php?t=619441
